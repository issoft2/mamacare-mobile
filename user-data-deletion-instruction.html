Facebook Data Deletion Instructions

If you signed into the Safeborn app using Facebook and wish to delete your account data, please follow these steps:

  1. Send an email to support@safeborn.com (or your dev email) with the subject line "Request Data Deletion".
  2. Include the email address or name associated with your Facebook login.
  3. Our team will permanently delete your account profile, pregnancy tracker history, and milestones from our databases within 48 hours and send you a confirmation email.
