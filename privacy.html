Privacy Policy (Nigeria)

Version: v1.0
Effective date: 2026-05-26
Region: Nigeria (NG)
Language: English

safeborn ("we", "us", "our") explains in this Privacy Policy how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information when you use our services.

This version is drafted for Nigeria-focused compliance, including NDPA 2023 and NDPR principles.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account data: name, email, phone, authentication identifiers.
  • Profile and health-related data you provide: pregnancy week, symptoms, logs, care-team details.
  • Device and usage data: app events, diagnostics, device model, operating system.
  • Communication data: support messages, care coordination messages.

2. Purposes of Processing

We process data to:

  • provide and maintain the safeborn service,
  • personalize care and educational guidance,
  • support safety and service quality,
  • manage account security and fraud prevention,
  • meet legal and regulatory obligations.

2.1. Marketing (with consent)

We may use your contact and usage data to send you updates, offers, or health tips. We only do this if you give explicit consent (opt-in). You can withdraw consent at any time in your privacy settings.

2.2. System Improvement (with consent)

We may use your data to improve our features, algorithms, and user experience. Where this is not essential for service delivery, we will ask for your consent. You can withdraw consent at any time.

2.3. Anonymous Commercialization (with consent)

We may use anonymized and aggregated data for research, analytics, or commercial purposes. This data cannot identify you. We only do this with your consent, and you can withdraw consent at any time.

2.4. Model Training (with consent)

We may use your data to train and improve AI models that power safeborn’s features. This is only done with your explicit consent, and you can withdraw consent at any time.

3. Legal Basis

Depending on context, we rely on:

  • consent,
  • contract necessity,
  • legal obligation,
  • legitimate interests balanced with your rights.

Where consent is required, you may withdraw it at any time.

4. Sensitive/Health Data

Health-related data may be sensitive personal data. We process this data with heightened safeguards and only where lawful grounds apply.

5. Sharing and Disclosure

We may share data with:

  • vetted processors/service providers,
  • care contacts you explicitly connect,
  • regulators or law enforcement when legally required,
  • professional advisors under confidentiality.

We do not sell your personal data.

6. Cross-Border Transfers

Where data is transferred outside Nigeria, we apply lawful safeguards (contractual or technical measures) and assess transfer risk.

7. Retention

We retain data only for as long as needed for service delivery, legal obligations, dispute handling, and security.

8. Your Rights

Subject to law, you may request:

  • access,
  • correction,
  • deletion,
  • restriction,
  • objection,
  • portability,
  • withdrawal of consent.

9. Security

We apply technical and organizational safeguards, including access controls, encryption in transit, and monitoring.

10. Children

If the service is used by or for minors, processing is handled under applicable child-data requirements.

11. Updates

We may release new versions of this Privacy Policy. Material updates may require renewed acknowledgment.

12. Contact
